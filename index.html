Welcome to a11yMTL 2015 - Accessibility Camp Montreal

In the spirit of active participation and to encourage networking, the a11yMTL team opted this year for a lighter formula, where informal sessions will be offered. Participants will be able to spontaneously volunteer that morning to present a paper, lead a discussion, run a workshop or simply mediate a group around a given topic.

Topics ideas to choose from:

  • Introduction to accessibility: A prospective approach to the future of the Web 
  • Test the accessibility of your website with NVDA 
  • Incorporate accessibility in the production cycle of your Web project 
  • The accessibility of PDF documents for the common man 
  • Share tips and tools to check availability

It will be an ideal opportunity to reflect collectively on issues of accessibility with actors of the web industry, experts and representatives of public and private organizations and representatives of various levels of government!

Join Web enthusiasts who will be there that day including:

  • Internal and external communications managers;
  • Information and communications technology specialists;
  • Decision makers and project managers from the public and private sectors;
  • Webmasters and content editors from the provincial and federal government levels;
  • Web craftsmen and women, who shape a more accessible Web each day.

Have access to coordinators, designers, developers, webmasters, consultants, trainers, managers and watchmen promoting best practice in accessibility. It is with the support of YOUR organization that this event continues its success.

Hope to see you there.

Please note that the site is wheelchair accessible.
